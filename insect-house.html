INSECT-HOUSE

The Insect House is a unique feature of our zoo, designed to provide a safe haven for various insect species. It serves as an educational tool, allowing visitors to learn about the importance of insects in our ecosystem. The house is filled with different habitats, showcasing the diversity of insect life and their roles in pollination and decomposition.

Visitors can observe insects in their natural environment, gaining a deeper appreciation for these often-overlooked creatures. The Insect House is not only a place of learning but also a reminder of the vital role insects play in maintaining the balance of nature.
